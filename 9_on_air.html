2. You can compare a selected story in different languages.One or more selected stories have been found and compared in different languages. (10)One or more selected stories have been found in different languages but the comparison was only partially done. (7,5)One or more stories have been found in different languages but no comparison was done. (5)No one story was found in another language. (2,5)
3. You can write the different parts of a news story in a clear, coherent and correct way.Your audio radio script includes its different parts and features and is clear, coherent and correct. (10)Your audio radio script includes its different parts and features but is not always clear, coherent and correct. (7,5)Your audio radio script includes its different parts and features but is often unclear, incoherent and incorrect. (5)Your audio radio script includes its different parts and but it does not include the features of an audio script. (2,5)
4. You can tell a piece of news from an opinion.You always spot the verbs and expressions that let you know when an opinion is expressed. (5)You never spot the verbs and expressions that let you know when an opinion is expressed. (2,5)
5. You can express opinions about the news.You always make use of the verbs and expressions that let opinions be expressed. (5)You never make use of the verbs and expressions that let opinions be expressed. (2,5)
6. You can create a radio outline.The outline includes timing, different roles and contents for a radio programme in a very cohesive way. (10)The outline includes timing, different roles and contents for a radio programme in a cohesive way most of the time. (7,5)The outline includes only some sections of a radio programme or it is sometimes not cohesive enough. (5)The outline does not include the sections of a radio programme or it is not cohesive. (2,5)
7. You can record and edit a radio programme.Your records are very clear and they are edited using digital resources and the outline with accuracy. (10)Your records are clear most of the time and they are edited using digital resources and the outline. (7,5)Your records are sometimes clear, and they are edited using digital resources and the outline with some inconsistencies. (5)Your records are not very clear, and its edition using digital resources does not follow the outline. (2,5)
